Building a Winning Product Roadmap

Product Management is about turning ideas into products that delight users. The roadmap guides the vision, prioritization, and execution strategy.

Steps to Build a Roadmap

  • Define Vision: Align with company strategy and user needs.
  • Identify Features: List key initiatives, prioritize based on impact.
  • Timeline & Milestones: Set realistic delivery dates.
  • Stakeholder Alignment: Communicate the plan clearly to all.
  • Iterate: Review and adjust based on feedback and market changes.

Pro Tips

Focus on user outcomes, not just features. Use metrics to guide prioritization and decision-making.
